Community Transport Guidelines

Safe • Reliable • Respectful Travel

Welcome to Belhopper Community Transport

To our new and existing clients — thank you for choosing us. Please take a moment to review our rules to ensure safe and smooth operations for everyone.

🚫 Not Allowed

  • No private trips outside the group system.
  • No lift clubs permitted.
  • No reckless rushing — safety always comes first.

📌 Booking Guidelines

  • Provide full pickup details when booking for others.
  • Next-day bookings must be made before 20:30.
  • Be ready on time — drivers wait up to 5 minutes.
  • Large groups (5+) must notify driver in advance.
  • Keep vehicles clean and respected.
  • Immediate payment required for EFT transfers.
  • Be ready in high-risk pickup zones for safety.
  • Some areas may be restricted based on safety conditions.

⚠️ Important Notice

If you cancel a next-day booking on the morning of the trip, half the fare will be charged.

Please cancel before your scheduled pickup time whenever possible.

💬 Communication

We value both feedback and complaints. Please report any issues or share compliments — we’re here to improve and serve you better.
