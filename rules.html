Rules & Guidelines

Keeping our community safe, reliable, and respectful

Welcome to BelHopper Community Transport

To our new and existing clients — thank you for choosing us. Please follow the rules below to ensure smooth and safe operations for everyone.

🚫 Not Allowed

  • No private trips outside the group system.
  • No lift clubs allowed.
  • No unsafe rushing — safety is priority.

📌 Booking Guidelines

  • Provide full pickup details when booking.
  • Next-day bookings must be done before 20:30.
  • Be ready on time — drivers wait max 5 minutes.
  • Large groups (5+) must inform driver in advance.
  • Keep vehicles clean and respected.
  • Immediate payment required for EFT transfers.
  • Be ready in high-risk areas.
  • Some areas may be restricted for safety reasons.

⚠️ Important Notice

If you cancel a next-day booking on the morning of the trip, half fare will be charged.

Please cancel before your scheduled time where possible.

💬 Feedback

We welcome both complaints and compliments. Your feedback helps us improve the service.
